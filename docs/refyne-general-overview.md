# Refyne - Platform Overview

## Product Vision

To be the essential **Community Growth Platform** for creators and brands. We provide the intelligent tools to **Protect**, **Understand**, **Engage**, and **Grow** your audience, turning your community into your most valuable asset.

## Target Audience

-   Instagram / Youtube creators
    
-   Small to medium-sized enterprises (SMEs)
    
-   Businesses managing active social media presence
    

## Meet Otto: Your AI Community Partner

At the heart of Refyne is **Otto**, our AI assistant. Otto is the friendly, efficient partner who works 24/7 to power the platform's four pillars, making your life easier and your community more valuable.

## The Four Pillars of Community Growth

### 1. Protect: Create a Safe & Positive Space
Otto acts as a calm guardian for your community.
-   **Context-Aware AI Moderation:** Goes beyond keywords to understand cultural nuance, slang, and brand-specific context, ensuring accurate filtering with fewer false positives.
-   **Real-time Filtering:** Instantly detects and hides spam, hate speech, and toxic comments.
-   **Custom Rulesets:** Define unique rules to block competitor mentions, sensitive topics, or any other content that doesn't fit your community guidelines.

### 2. Understand: Gain Actionable Audience Insights
Otto finds the signal in the noise, helping you make smarter decisions.
-   **Deep Sentiment Analysis:** Get real-time insights into how your audience feels about your content, campaigns, and brand.
-   **Trend & Keyword Detection:** Discover the key topics and themes driving conversation within your community.
-   **Predictive Insights:** Proactively get suggestions from Otto on how to improve engagement and maintain community health.

### 3. Engage: Build Loyalty on Autopilot
Otto helps you build relationships at scale.
-   **Automated Comment Replies:** Set up instant, helpful responses to frequently asked questions.
-   **Celebrate Your Fans:** Automatically identify and engage with your most positive and loyal supporters.
-   **Smart Actions:** Auto-pin top comments to highlight positivity and keep the best conversations going.

### 4. Grow: Turn Engagement into Results
Otto helps you connect community activity to business growth.
-   **Automated Lead Capture:** Use our visual workflow builder to turn interested commenters into qualified leads in your DMs.
-   **DM Sales Funnels:** Guide potential customers from a comment to a purchase through automated, personalized conversations.
-   **CRM & Email Integration:** Seamlessly send new leads and customer data to your existing marketing tools.

## Technical Architecture

-   **Frontend:** Next.js (hosted on Vercel)
    
-   **Backend:** Golang microservices architecture (Gin framework)
    
-   **AI Integrations:** Google Gemini
    
-   **Database:** PostgreSQL (user metadata), Redis (cache)
    
-   **Security:** JWT authentication, HTTPS, secure API key management
    
## User Journey (Sample)

### Influencer Perspective (Emma, Lifestyle Blogger)

1.  Signs up and connects her Instagram account. Otto greets her and helps set up her "protective bubble."
2.  Emma teaches Otto about her brand, specifying friendly competitors and topics she wants to avoid.
3.  She immediately sees a cleaner, more positive comment section.
4.  Otto sends her a weekly insight report: "Your audience loved the behind-the-scenes content this week! Positive sentiment was 30% higher on those posts."
5.  Based on Otto's suggestion, she sets up an auto-reply for the most common question: "Where is your jacket from?"

### SME Business Owner Perspective (John, SaaS Founder)

1.  Implements Refyne and uses the dashboard to understand that many potential customers are asking about pricing in his comments.
2.  John uses the visual workflow builder to create an automation:
    -   **Trigger:** Otto detects a comment containing "pricing" or "how much."
    -   **Action 1:** Otto auto-replies publicly: "Good question! I've sent you a DM with the details."
    -   **Action 2:** Otto sends a private DM: "Hey! Here's a link to our pricing page. If you have any more questions, just ask here!"
3.  John's sales team now receives qualified leads directly from their social media comments, and the community gets instant, helpful answers.
