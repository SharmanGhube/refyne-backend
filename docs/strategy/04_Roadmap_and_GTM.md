# 04: Refyne Roadmap & Go-to-Market Strategy

This document outlines the phased development roadmap and the strategy for bringing Refyne to market.

## 1. Phased Implementation Roadmap

### Phase 1: MVP - The Foundation (Target: 3 Months)
**Goal:** Launch a stable product that solves core moderation and basic engagement needs.
-   **Features:**
    -   **Protect:** AI Toxic Comment Detection, Spam & Bot Detection, Custom Rulesets.
    -   **Understand:** Basic Sentiment Analysis.
    -   **Engage:** Simple Keyword-Based Auto-Replies.
    -   **Platform:** Authentication, User Profiles.

### Phase 2: Intelligence & Advanced Engagement (Target: Months 4-6)
**Goal:** Deepen the platform's intelligence and expand engagement tools to increase user retention.
-   **Features:**
    -   **Protect:** Contextual Filtering, Manual Review Mode.
    -   **Understand:** Brand Safety Score, Weekly Summary Emails.
    -   **Engage:** Auto-Pin Top Comments.
    -   **Platform:** Cross-Platform Support (add YouTube).

### Phase 3: The Growth Engine (Target: Months 7-9)
**Goal:** Introduce the `Grow` pillar, transforming the platform into a revenue-generating engine.
-   **Features:**
    -   **Grow:** Visual Automation Builder, DM Chatbot for lead capture, CRM/Email integrations.
    -   **Platform:** Team Roles & Permissions for agencies.

### Phase 4: Scale & Enterprise (Target: Months 10-12+)
**Goal:** Solidify market position with enterprise-grade features and predictive analytics.
-   **Features:**
    -   **Understand:** Pre-Publish Risk Scan.
    -   **Platform:** White-Label Dashboards, Public API & Webhooks, Custom AI Model Training.

## 2. Go-to-Market (GTM) Strategy

### Target Audience (Initial)
-   **Primary:** Instagram creators with 10k-100k followers who are overwhelmed by comment management.
-   **Secondary:** Small social media agencies managing a handful of creator accounts.

### Pricing Strategy (Initial)
-   **Free Tier:** Limited to 1,000 comments/month with basic moderation.
-   **Creator Plan ($29/month):** Full MVP feature set for a single social account.
-   **Agency Plan ($99/month):** Adds team features and support for multiple social accounts.

### Launch & Marketing Strategy
1.  **Content Marketing:**
    -   Launch a blog series focused on "Creator Mental Health" and the "Creator Economy."
    -   Publish a "State of Online Comment Toxicity 2025" report to generate backlinks and press.
2.  **Influencer Partnerships:**
    -   Partner with 20 mid-tier creators, offering free lifetime access in exchange for authentic testimonials and case studies.
    -   Sponsor creator-focused newsletters and podcasts.
3.  **Community Building:**
    -   Create a Discord server for users to share best practices for building healthy online communities.
    -   Use Refyne on our own social media channels to demonstrate its power (dogfooding).
4.  **Product Hunt Launch:**
    -   Plan a strategic launch on Product Hunt to tap into the early adopter community.

## 3. Suggestions & Improvements
-   **Freemium to Paid Conversion:** The key is to make the free tier valuable enough to prove the concept, but limited enough (by comment volume or advanced features) that active creators will quickly see the need to upgrade.
-   **Focus on a Niche:** For the initial launch, consider focusing on a specific creator vertical (e.g., gaming, beauty, or finance) to tailor messaging and build a strong beachhead before expanding.
